Who Are We

Here at Good Samaritan, we strive to provide a reliable platform where people can donate or offer services to those in need. Our main source of assistance accompasses organizing resources and searching for public events that require the use of volunteers.

What Are We Trying to Accomplish

Our main goal is making it easier for those in need to seek help. They have access to our list of available resources.
